Frequently Asked Questions

We offer money back guarantee - If you're not happy with your store for any reason, you can request your money back.

What is dropshipping?

Dropshipping is an e-commerce model where a store doesn't keep the products it sells in stock. Instead, when a store sells a product, a third party supplier ships it directly to the customer. As a result, the merchant never sees or handles the product.

What happens after I purchase a store?

- When you purchase one of our packages, we will provide you with a list of profitable niches to choose from.
- Then, we'll suggest the most profitable products in that niche, which you can replace at any time.
- Once you've approved the products, we'll help you with your business name and create your store and other assets.
- Once your store is approved, we will transfer full ownership to you and provide step-by-step instructions, including video tutorials, on how to start selling and advertising your products.

Suppliers location?

We work with suppliers that have warehouses in multiple countries to enable global sales, faster shipping times, and lower product costs. The average worldwide shipping time is typically 7-12 days, with most products arriving in 5-10 days in the US, UK, CA, and AU, which is highly efficient for dropshipping.

Lifetime support?

We offer lifetime support to all our clients! Our lifetime support and guidance include providing answers and guidance to any questions you might have about your store, advertising, or dropshipping in general.

Can I add more products?

Yes, you can add more products after we create and hand over the store to you, or hire us to add more products for you at any time. There's no limit to the number of products that can be added to the store, and your store will be 100% customizable from your end.

Are there any other fees?

Strategy Fox doesn't charge any ongoing fees apart from a one-time payment when you order a package from our website. The other fees you may expect are a Shopify monthly subscription of $32 and a domain name registration of $12 per year.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Yes, our packages are designed to be beginner-friendly, with step-by-step guidance and lifetime support even after your store is created. We provide thorough documentation and instructions for running your store and ads successfully. We've worked with clients who had no prior experience, and they now run successful stores.
